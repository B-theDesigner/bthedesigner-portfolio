Lead Product Designer 6 Months 8+ stakeholders xx% Engagament xx% Adoption 10+ User Interviews Web Responsive Mobile App

The Problem

Users struggled to understand the status of their verification requests, leading to confusion, repeat submissions, and increased support volume.

The Challenge

  • Multiple user types with different needs
  • Highly regulated content
  • 10+ conditional document flows
  • Legacy system contraints

My Approach

  • Audited existing flows to identify friction points
  • Simplified request states into a unified model
  • Designed a scalable verification center pattern
  • Validated concepts through moderated usability testing

The Outcome

  • ↑ 28% task completion rate
  • ↓ duplicate submissions
  • Positive feedback from internal support teams
  • Pattern adopted across additional use cases
